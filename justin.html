About Justin

The Journey of a Superstar

Justin Drew Bieber is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist. Discovered at age 13 by talent manager Scooter Braun after he saw Bieber's YouTube cover songs, Bieber was signed to RBMG Records in 2008.

With worldwide sales of over 150 million records, Bieber is one of the best-selling music artists of all time. He has won numerous awards, including two Grammy Awards, one Latin Grammy Award, and holds several Guinness World Records.

Bieber's first album, "My World 2.0" (2010), debuted at number one in several countries and included the hit single "Baby". His later albums include "Purpose" (2015), "Changes" (2020), and "Justice" (2021).
